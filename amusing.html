坐标：宁波鼓楼 大娃古着店 Dawa Vintage Store

营业时间：12:00～21:00

Business hours：12:00～21:00

出售：古着类服饰 首饰 眼镜 帽子 包包

Sale: Vintage clothing / jewelry / glasses / hat / bag

开在鼓楼巷子里的一个低调的角落，走进去像是拾起多年没有找到的少女梦，五彩斑斓，十分梦幻。店里整体风格颜色搭配都非常亮眼，衣服款式新质量好，购入渠道由泰日韩为主，也有自营品牌。性价比很高，学生可以放心去逛。店内有试衣间和适合拍照的复古全身镜，也很方便出片。去感受可可爱爱奇奇怪怪的vintage吧！
Open in a humble corner of one alley of Gulou. Walking into it is like picking up the girl's dream that has not been found for many years. Colorful and dreamy. The overall style and color of the store are very eye-catching. The clothing is comparatively new with good quality. Its purchase channel is mainly from Thailand, Japan and South Korea, and there are also self-owned brands. Cost-effective, students can rest assured to go shopping. There is a fitting room and retro full-length mirror suitable for taking photos. Go and feel the lovely and strange Vintage!

坐标：PHOTO BOOTH 海曙区府桥街114号（鼓楼附近）

Coordinates: Photo Booth, 114 Fuqiao street, Haishu District (near Gulou)

价格：36元/组 可拍4张

Price: 36 RMB / group for 4 pieces

韩剧《请回答1988》同款大头贴！和朋友/情侣都可以体验。 超多款式的头套、眼镜和发箍可供选择，选好就可以进小房间拍摄。全自动机器，相纸、背景颜色、滤镜都可以自己选择，每张倒计时间隔十秒，拍之前要和小伙伴商量好合照姿势哦！拍完即刻出片，不用等待。
The same style photobooth as South Korean drama "Reply 1988"! Experience it with friends/lovers. A plethora of headgears, eyeglasses and headbands are available to choose. Fully automatic machine in a small room. Free to choose the photo paper, background color and filter by yourself. Each countdown interval is 10 seconds. You can get the photo right away.
