薄皮酱香妙龄鸡
The young chicken is spiced with thin skin
¥28 推荐指数：🌟🌟🌟🌟🌟
每天只卖一百份的店内招牌！一整只童子鸡下单现炸，呈金黄色，外皮炸得酥脆，配的是韩式甜辣酱，旁边还配有蜂蜜芥末酱和番茄酱可供选择，肉非常嫩，一口咬下去还能爆汁，还有炸得香脆的薯条。
Only one hundred servings a day! A whole spring chicken was freshly fried, golden brown and crispy on the outside, served in a Korean sweet chili sauce, with honey mustard and tomato sauce on the side. The meat was tender and juicy enough to be eaten in one bite, as well as crispy fries on the side.
咸蛋黄炸鸡年糕拉面
Salted egg yolk fried chicken rice cake ramen
¥23 推荐指数：🌟🌟🌟🌟
整块咸蛋黄鸡排铺在拉面上，超级诱人！鸡排嫩得像鱼肉，撒着紫菜碎，拉面会有一点点辣，份量不多，几口就能解决，要趁热吃哦，冷了面的口感会变差。
The whole piece of salted egg chicken chop is placed on the ramen, super attractive! The chicken chop is as tender as cooked fish and sprinkled with shredded laver. Ramen will be a little spicy, and a few mouthfuls can be solved. Pay attention to eat while it is hot, or cold noodles will taste worse.
甜辣年糕丁
Sweet and spicy rice cake cubes
¥6 推荐指数：🌟🌟🌟🌟
真的会滑动的小推车模型哦！韩式年糕炸得软糯适中，切成丁的形状，上面淋着韩式甜辣酱，牙签插着两面小国旗，看起来爆有食欲。
A really sliding trolley model! Korean rice cakes cut into diced shape, are fried soft and glutinous, covered with sweet chili sauce with toothpicks topped with two small national flags. It looks super appetizing!
葡萄颗颗爽
Grape bubble juice
¥14 推荐指数：🌟🌟🌟🌟
莫吉托鸡尾酒的味道！底部是青葡萄果肉，上面是气泡水，喝起来像果啤。刚好可以解吃鸡的腻。美中不足是有点小贵～
The taste of mojito! On the bottom is the flesh of the green grape, and on the top is the sparkling water, which tastes like fruit beer. Just the thing to get rid of the chicken. The fly in the ointment is that the price is a little expensive ~
